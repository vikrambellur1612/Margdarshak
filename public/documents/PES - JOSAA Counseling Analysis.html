üîç Key Insights for Admission Planning

  • Rank Variability: Even at high percentiles (99.5+), PES ranks can vary significantly due to exam normalization and other factors.
  • Campus Difference: RR Campus generally has 500-1000 better ranks than EC Campus for same branches.
  • CSE Competitiveness: CSE at RR Campus needs rank under 1403, while EC Campus accepts till rank 3752.
  • AIML Popularity: AI/ML combinations have slightly lower cutoffs than pure CSE.
  • Safe Range: For guaranteed admission to some engineering branch, rank should be under 6000.
  • Percentile Threshold: 95+ percentile gives good chances for top branches, 90+ for decent branches.
