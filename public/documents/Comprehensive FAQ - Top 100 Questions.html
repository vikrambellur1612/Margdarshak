17. Is there any particular strategy to clear JEE Mains? What guidelines would be helpful for students?

🏆 Comprehensive JEE Main Success Strategy:

📚 Subject-wise Preparation Strategy

⚗️ Chemistry (Easiest to Score)

  • NCERT First: 70% questions directly from NCERT
  • Inorganic: Pure memory-based, create flashcards
  • Organic: Practice mechanism and name reactions
  • Physical: Formula-based, practice numerical problems
  • Target: 25-28 correct out of 30 questions

📐 Mathematics (High Scoring Potential)

  • High Priority Topics: Coordinate Geometry, Calculus, Algebra
  • Practice Strategy: Solve 10-15 problems daily per topic
  • Speed Building: Time yourself for each question type
  • Target: 22-25 correct out of 30 questions

⚛️ Physics (Most Challenging)

  • Conceptual Clarity: Understand principles before formula memorization
  • High Weightage: Mechanics, Electrodynamics, Modern Physics
  • Numerical Practice: Focus on problem-solving techniques
  • Target: 20-22 correct out of 30 questions

⏰ Time Management Strategy

Subject Recommended Time Strategy
Chemistry 45-50 minutes Start here for confidence building
Mathematics 75-80 minutes Attempt familiar topics first
Physics 70-75 minutes Skip tough numericals initially
Review 10-15 minutes Double-check marked questions

📈 Monthly Preparation Plan

  • Months 1-2: NCERT completion + basic problem solving
  • Months 3-4: Reference books + previous year papers
  • Months 5-6: Mock tests + weak area focus
  • Last Month: Revision + mock test analysis
🎯 Scoring Strategy: Aim for 67-70 correct answers out of 90 (75-78% accuracy) for 200+ score. Focus on accuracy over attempting all questions.
