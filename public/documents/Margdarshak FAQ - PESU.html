Will there be any separate entrance exam for admission to PES University?
There used to be an entrance exam called PESSAT (PES Scholastic Aptitude Test) using which admissions happened for the batch 2024-25 and earlier batches.

PESSAT is being no longer held from the Academic Year 2025-26 for B.Tech admissions.
What is the process for admission to PESU?
To be considered in PESU admission process one needs to do registration through their website (Registration Link: Online Registration Form (PES University)). This registration is to be done by all except those taking admissions through the Karnataka Government KCET counselling.

Registration Fee: ‚Çπ1200 per mode. For 2025-26 admissions, PESU had two registrations mode ‚Äì JEE Main and KCET. One may select more than one modes during registration. The PESU KCET registration mode has a different meaning than KEA KCET Government quota.

Parents are requested to check for more updated/relevant information at the official website of PESU and registration link shared above.
What is the breakup for seats for various admission modes to PESU?
  • 40% seats are allocated for admissions on the basis of KCET through the Karnataka Government KEA KCET counselling
  • 45% seats are allocated for JEE Main and KCET quota through the PESU direct counselling
  • 15% seats are allocated for Higher Fee Category / Management Quota

Actual breakup of number of seats per campus per branch valid for 2026-27 admissions will be known at the time of counselling process as the intake may change for a particular year. For 2025-26 admitted batch, the breakup can be seen at - https://pes.edu/admissions/program-intake
What are the fee details for various admission modes to PES?
  • KEA KCET counselling (40% quota) - approx. 1.25L per year
  • PESU direct counselling (45% quota) - approx. 4L to 5L per year
  • Higher Fee Category / Management Quota (15% quota) - For Higher Fee Category / Management / NRI / PIO / OCI / Sponsored Seats, one has to contact the Office of Admissions at PES University or write to admissions@pes.edu

Exact fee details will be known at the time of counselling process which varies as per the branch alloted / chosen and if there is an increase in fee for a particular year.
What is PES University and how is it different from other private colleges?
PES University (PESU) is one of the most sought-after private engineering colleges in Karnataka, typically grouped with RVCE, BMS, and MSRIT as top-tier institutions. It's often compared to VIT Vellore and MIT Manipal nationally. PES has never participated in COMEDK counseling and conducts its own admission process.
What are the different campuses of PES University?
PES has two main campuses:
  • Ring Road (RR) Campus: The original and flagship campus, historically preferred by students with higher ranks
  • Electronic City (EC) Campus: Newer campus with same faculty and curriculum as RR campus
Both campuses belong to the same university (PESU) with shared faculty, same syllabus, and joint placement activities.
What are the admission routes for PES University?
PES has three main admission categories:
  • KCET Quota (40% seats): Through KEA counseling, fees ~‚Çπ1.25L per annum
  • PES Counseling (45% seats): Through PES's own counseling based on JEE Main scores / KCET rank - fees ‚Çπ4-5L per annum
  • Management Quota (15% seats): Direct admission with higher fees
Has PESSAT been discontinued?
Yes, PES discontinued PESSAT from 2025. They now admit students based on JEE Main scores, KCET ranks, and COMEDK scores through their own counseling process. This change was made to attract students from across India rather than just Bangalore-based students.
How does PES admission work through JEE quota?
Students must register on the PES website (registration fee ‚Çπ1200). PES conducts its own counseling based on JEE Main ranks - they DO NOT participate in JOSAA counseling. About 45% of seats are filled through this route with fees ranging from ‚Çπ4-5L per annum.
Can KCET students apply for both quotas in PES?
Yes, KCET students have two options:
  • Apply through KEA counseling (no PES registration needed) - fees ‚Çπ1.25L per annum
  • Register with PES for their counseling using KCET rank - fees ‚Çπ4-5L per annum
About 10% of the JEE quota seats may be allotted to KCET/COMEDK rank holders who register with PES.
What happens if I get a seat through both PES counseling and KEA counseling?
If you secure a seat through PES counseling (higher fee) and later get the same specialization and campus through KEA counseling (lower fee), PES will refund the difference amount. You can then continue with the lower fee structure.
When does PES counseling happen?
PES typically conducts their first round of counseling around May (before JOSAA counseling), with subsequent rounds aligning with KCET counseling timeline. The exact dates are announced after JEE Main results.
What are the typical JEE cutoffs for PES?
While PES doesn't publish official cutoffs, based on parent experiences:
  • Computer Science (RR Campus): JEE Main rank around 8,000-12,000
  • Computer Science (EC Campus): JEE Main rank around 15,000-20,000
  • Other branches: Varies based on demand and supply
The actual cutoff depends on the number of applicants and their willingness to pay the higher fees.
How are placements at PES University?
PES has a strong placement record:
  • Percentage-wise placement is better than VIT due to smaller intake
  • Good placement opportunities in IT companies
  • Average packages competitive with other top private colleges
  • Both campuses have joint placement activities
However, like all colleges, placements can be affected by industry conditions.
Which campus is better - Ring Road or Electronic City?
Both campuses have the same faculty, curriculum, and belong to PESU. However:
  • RR Campus is the original campus with established legacy
  • Higher rankers typically prefer RR campus during counseling
  • EC Campus is newer but equally equipped
  • Academic quality and placements are comparable
Choice often depends on personal preference and availability.
What documents are needed for PES registration?
Basic documents typically include:
  • Class 10th and 12th mark sheets
  • JEE Main scorecard (if applicable)
  • KCET scorecard (if applicable)
  • Valid email ID and phone number (can be parent's)
  • Passport-size photograph
Check the official PES website for complete and updated requirements.
How does PES compare to other colleges like VIT, RVCE, BMS?
PES is typically grouped with RVCE, BMS, and MSRIT as top private colleges in Bangalore. Key points:
  • More diverse student population due to no state quota
  • Smaller intake compared to VIT, leading to better percentage placement
  • Strong industry connections and research partnerships
  • Located in Bangalore, providing better internship and job opportunities
  • Fees are higher than government quota seats but comparable to other private colleges
Is it too early to register for PES in October?
It's not too early as PES allows early registration. The final admission is based on merit (JEE/KCET scores) and not on registration date. However, ensure you keep your registration details updated once exam scores are available.
What are the additional fees apart from tuition?
Apart from the tuition fees, students typically pay:
  • Registration fee: ‚Çπ1200 (for JEE quota)
  • Additional college fees: ‚Çπ25,000-50,000 (as reported by parents)
  • Hostel fees: Separate charges if opting for accommodation
Total cost can vary based on the quota and additional services chosen.
Can I change my branch or campus after admission?
PES allows sliding to a more preferred specialization/campus if you become eligible in subsequent counseling rounds based on your JEE/KCET scores. However, this is subject to seat availability and their counseling rules.
Important: PES admission processes and fee structures can change each year. Always verify current information from the official PES University website and admission notifications.
