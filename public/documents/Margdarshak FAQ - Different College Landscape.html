PES University (PESU)

1. What major changes has PES made for admissions?

PES has discontinued PESSAT and now considers JEE/KCET/COMEDK scores for admissions. The college maintains two admission channels: • 40% seats through KCET counseling (fees around ₹

1. 25L per annum via KEA) • 45% seats through their own counseling based on JEE/KCET/COMEDK ranks (fees ₹4-5L per annum) 2. How does PES counseling work?

PES conducts its own counseling process separate from centralized counseling: • Round-1 typically happens before KCET/COMEDK counseling • Full fee payment required on counseling day with physical presence • Round-2 happens after other counseling processes • Interestingly, Round-2 cutoXs can sometimes be higher than Round-1 due to demand-supply dynamics

3. What are the fee adjustment policies at PES?

• Students who secure PES seats through higher-fee counseling but later get KCET seats can get fee adjustments • Full refund policies follow UGC guidelines with time-based deductions • Withdrawal before Round-2 counseling typically allowed with minimal deduction (around ₹10K) RV College Ecosystem
