WhatsApp Group Structure & Guidelines

About This Guide: This document explains the purpose of various WhatsApp groups that are part of the Margdarshak 25-26 Community. Though group names are intuitive, please go through the detailed description of each group to understand its purpose.

Important: Please enroll yourself in all the relevant groups and post your messages in the appropriate groups only.

üè´ Marg 25-26 Engg - VIT/ MIT/ PESU/ MHCET/ Others

This group is for discussion about admission to all engineering colleges, other than those that are clearly called out in the two other engineering groups.

This includes VIT, MIT, Amrita, Sastra, Shiv Nadar, MHCET, TNEA

This includes their entrance exams, counselling/ admission process.

We are aware that there are some institutions (notably PESU) which use the JEE Main score. So in such cases, keep the discussion about the JEE Main exams in the other group, and anything related to how PESU or other colleges use it in this group.

This also includes things like Tamil Nadu counselling where there are no entrance exams.
