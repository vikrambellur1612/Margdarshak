COMEDK Engineering Admissions – Extensive FAQ

Based on in-group chat discussions only • Last updated Jan 2025

  1. What is COMEDK and how is it different from KCET?
    COMEDK is an all-India entrance test (no domicile restriction) for private engineering colleges in Karnataka; KCET is for Karnataka residents only (7 years of study in Karnataka, with 10th or 12th there). KCET rank = 50% board PCM marks + 50% KCET exam; COMEDK uses only exam performance for ranking. Fees: KCET ~₹1.2L/yr; COMEDK ~₹3L/yr.
  2. Who can apply for COMEDK?
    Anyone with Indian nationality, including NRI/OCI, who has passed (or is appearing in) 12th grade (PCM + English).
  3. What are the main routes for private engineering college admissions in Karnataka?
    (1) KCET (state students only); (2) COMEDK (all-India); (3) Management quota (direct to college, higher fees); some colleges/universities admit via their own entrance, but recent policy aligns all with govt exams.
  4. Can non-Karnataka students get CSE at top colleges (RVCE, BMS, PES etc)?
    Yes, via COMEDK or management quota (not KCET).
  5. What’s the difference among RVCE, RVU, and RVITM?
    RVCE: “Flagship”, oldest, VTU-autonomous, all branches, top placements. RVU: New, private university, mainly BTech (Hons) in CS, not VTU. RVITM: New, VTU (not autonomous), lower cutoff, separate faculty/placements.
  6. Are placements and faculty shared across RVCE, RVU, RVITM?
    No, all independent; only occasional shared placement drives, but selection/final results are distinct.
  7. How do KCET, COMEDK, and management quota fees compare?
    KCET (~₹1.2L/yr); COMEDK (~₹3L/yr); Management/JEE routes in private colleges: ₹4–5L/yr+.
  8. If I join via management/JEE and later secure KCET/COMEDK, is the excess fee refunded?
    Varies by college, but PES and RVCE allow excess fee adjustment/refund if moved to a lower-fee seat within deadlines.
  9. What is UNI-GAUGE and how does it differ from COMEDK?
    It’s an optional PAN-India score (including some Karnataka private univs). Same exam, separate rank. Most use COMEDK, unless targeting UNI-GAUGE colleges.
  10. Do Class 12 board marks affect COMEDK ranking?
    No—eligibility only; rank = entrance exam score only.
  11. Do board marks affect KCET rank?
    Yes, KCET rank = 50% KCET + 50% PCM (board exam, any board).
  12. Is BTech (Hons) from RVU equivalent to a BTech from a VTU college?
    BTech (Hons) means extra courses/credits; value in placements/higher education largely depends on brand/alumni network and individual accomplishments, not degree title per se.
  13. What is the COMEDK exam structure?
    180 MCQs (Phy 60, Chem 60, Math 60), 3 hours, no negative marking.
  14. How do I apply for management quota at RVCE, BMS, PES?
    Directly via college; applications open early, seats/fees vary, must meet minimum eligibility.
  15. Which colleges participate in COMEDK counseling?
    Most top Karnataka private engineering colleges (e.g. RVCE, BMSCE, MSRIT, BNMIT, NHCE, CMRIT, RVITM, etc.).
  16. Can I write both KCET and COMEDK?
    Yes, and it’s common to do so.
  17. How does the seat allocation/counseling process work?
    After results, branch/college preferences are entered online; seat allotted on rank/preference; multiple rounds occur.
  18. How do I check KCET/COMEDK seat cutoffs?
    On official COMEDK and KEA websites every year; look up last admitted rank by branch/college.
  19. If I take COMEDK + UNI-GAUGE, is it two exams or one?
    One exam, two ranks, if you register for both.
  20. BE vs BTech—any practical difference?
    For jobs/PG, little to no difference; BTech may be a bit more practical/applied, but overlap is huge.
  21. Can I switch from COMEDK/management seat to KCET if I get a better allotment later?
    Policies differ; most good colleges (PES, RVCE) allow it with fee adjustment (within deadlines).
  22. When does COMEDK application open?
    Usually February; exam is typically in May.
  23. Is there a domicile quota under COMEDK?
    No; open to all.
  24. How are COMEDK counseling rounds structured?
    Multiple rounds—initial, surrendered/vacant seat rounds, each with new cutoffs.
  25. What are the refund rules if withdrawing after taking a COMEDK seat?
    Usually UGC rules (e.g., ₹10k deduction pre-2nd round), but college-specific policies prevail—read offer terms carefully.
  26. Is COMEDK exam pattern and level different from UNI-GAUGE?
    No, they’re identical if you select both.
  27. Is COMEDK easier/harder than KCET/JEE?
    Slightly easier than JEE Main; on par or a bit tougher than KCET; speed is crucial.
  28. Can NRIs/OCIs apply for COMEDK?
    Yes.
  29. Do all private Karnataka engineering colleges accept COMEDK?
    Nearly all do, but always check the year’s official list; some (like PES University) are exceptions.
  30. Can international school students apply to COMEDK?
    Yes, as long as +2 PCM and English requirements (and minimum marks) are met.
